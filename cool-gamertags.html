What Makes a Gamertag Cool?

A cool gamertag sounds strong, looks clean, and is easy to remember. It avoids cliches like xXx wrappers, long number strings, and random character spam. The best cool names combine two strong words in PascalCase with an optional short number.

Tips for Picking a Cool Name

  1. Short beats long: IronWolf is cooler than TheGreatIronWolfWarrior
  2. Hard consonants sound strong: K, T, X, V, and R make names punch harder (StrikeViper vs. SoftBreeze)
  3. Avoid overused words: Killer, Sniper, and Pro are in millions of names. Stand out with less common words
  4. No special characters: xXx, underscores, and periods look dated. Clean PascalCase is the modern standard
