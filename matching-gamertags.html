How Matching Gamertags Work

Matching gamertags share a common word, theme, or structure so people can tell you play together. The most common pattern is keeping one word the same and swapping the other. For example: ShadowKnight and ShadowMage share "Shadow" but have different roles.

Matching Gamertag Patterns

Use these formulas to create your own matching pair:

  1. Same adjective, different noun: ShadowWolf & ShadowHawk
  2. Same noun, different adjective: IronKnight & FrostKnight
  3. Opposites: FireStorm & IceStorm, DarkBlade & LightBlade
  4. Same name, different number: VoidHunter01 & VoidHunter02
  5. Role-based: StormSword & StormShield, ApexSniper & ApexMedic
  6. His/Hers or King/Queen: any base word with a gendered pair suffix

Tips for Matching Pairs

  1. Check both names are available on your platform before committing to either one
  2. Keep the same length so they look balanced on scoreboards
  3. Use the same number (or no number) for visual consistency
  4. Avoid inside jokes that only you two understand if you want other people to recognize the pair
