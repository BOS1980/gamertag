Tips for Picking a Popular Gamertag

  1. Keep it short: 8-16 characters read well in scoreboards and chat.
  2. Use numbers wisely: Add a number at the end if your preferred name is taken. Avoid long number strings.
  3. Match the vibe: A horror game player with a humor tag stands out, but a power tag builds credibility in ranked modes.
  4. Test readability: PascalCase (ShadowKing) is easier to read than all-lowercase (shadowking).
  5. Check availability: Try your chosen name on your target platform before committing.
