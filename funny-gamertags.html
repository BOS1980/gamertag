Tips for Funny Names That Do Not Get Banned

  1. Use wordplay and absurdity, not shock value
  2. Avoid anything that could be read as targeting a specific group
  3. Test it: would you laugh if you saw this name in a lobby? Or would you report it?
  4. Food, animals, and everyday objects are always safe comedy material
  5. Oxymorons work great: GentleBrute, TacticalPotato, StealthElephant
